Sabbath Paranoid
by Toto

Sabbath Paranoid is based on the lettering found on the sleeve of
Black Sabbath's Paranoid album released on September 1970. See 
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paranoid_(album) for more info about
Black Sabbath's Paranoid album.

Sabbath Paranoid is free for personal, private, non-commercial use.
